Beautiful isometric & 2D diagrams, right in your browser

Axoview is a free, open-source diagramming tool for cloud architecture, network topologies and infrastructure maps — with built-in AWS, GCP, Azure and Kubernetes icon packs. No install, no sign-up.

What you can build

A diagramming canvas built for infrastructure

From a single service sketch to a full multi-view architecture, Axoview keeps it fast, editable and presentable.

Isometric & 2D

Toggle between crisp isometric and flat 2D views of the same diagram — your zoom and centre are preserved.

Cloud icon packs

AWS, GCP, Azure, Kubernetes and core packs are built in, so infrastructure diagrams come together in minutes.

Rich editing

Multi-select, copy/paste, undo/redo, connectors, floating labels, inline rich text and a docked style strip.

Multi-view diagrams

Multiple named views per file, cross-diagram links, and layers — organise complex systems without clutter.

Present & export

A distraction-free present mode with an item-info popover, plus robust PNG/image export with presets.

Fast at scale

A WebGL2 canvas holds 60 fps while panning diagrams with thousands of nodes.

Why Axoview

Open, private, and yours to host

Free & open-source

A community fork of FossFLOW and Isoflow, with expanded editing, file management and full internationalisation.

Your data stays yours

Work saves to your browser, or to your own Google Drive if you connect it. Axoview only opens and saves the diagrams you make here — it can't see any of your other files in Google Drive, and nothing is ever kept on our servers. No tracking, no analytics.

Self-hostable

Run it yourself with Docker, or use the hosted app. Same build, deploy it your way.
